By Train

It takes a day to get to Ilic from Istanbul Haydarpasa by train, a train leaves station everyday at 08:30 it arrives at Ankara at 18:00.

By Bus

It also takes a day to get to Ilic by bus, buses are scheduled to leave on sundays at 14:00.

Firat Seyahat Tel : 0212 222 29 86 , 0212 222 42 32

By Air

Turkish Airlines has flights to Erzincan, which is located 117 Km north of the village, visitors can take the train from the city to Ilic.
